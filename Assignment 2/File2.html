REVIEW OF GAME OF THRONES

Game of Thrones is a fantasy television series based on George R.R. Martin's book series A Song of Ice and Fire.It aired from 2011 to 2019 over eight seasons, and it became a cultural phenomenon for its complex characters, intricate political plots, and epic battles.
